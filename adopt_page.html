Pet gallery

Can't decide yet what type of four-legged critter you want to take home with you? Don't worry, at the Blackbrook pet shelter we have a wide variety of dogs and cats.
Just keep in mind that most dogs require upwards of two hours of exercise per day, while cats can spend as much as fourteen hours a day sleeping. To have a look at all available pets just click anywhere on this card and you'll be taken to the pet gallery.

Adopt a dog

Are you looking to adopt a dog? That's great news, but make sure to choose a dog that's a good fit with you and your lifestyle. At the Blackbrook pet shelter we're happy to help you find the perfect match.
Our staff members know each dog like the back of their hand, so make sure to leverage their expertise. To have a look at all available dogs just click anywhere on this card and you'll be taken to the dog gallery

Adopt a cat

Are you looking to adopt a cat? We are happy to hear that! There are many types of cats, but which one is the right fit for you? Would you prefer a bundle of energy, or perhaps the feline equivalent of a couch potato? At the Blackbrook pet shelter we'd be happy to help you find the perfect match. To have a look at all available cats just click anywhere on this card and you'll be taken to the cat gallery
