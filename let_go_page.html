Letting go of your pet

Sometimes circumstances in life make it impossible for you or your loved ones to continue taking care of your pet. The good news is that we can step in by providing that care, and quickly find new owners for most animals. Nobody is more aware than us that parting ways with your pet is not an easy choice to make, but sometimes it's the only option. You can rely on us to give them all the care and attention they need until a good new home is found. If you need to let go of a pet, then please don't hesitate to fill in the contact form to get in touch with us. At the Blackbrook pet shelter, we pride ourselves on the professional and, above all, loving way that your pet is cared for while waiting for a new home. We are also very experienced with animals that have medical or behavioral issues, so any type of pet is in good hands with us.
